### Overview
Provisional Pages are pages that have not yet been promoted into a [[1. Canonical Page]] via the minimum thresholds defined in [[Canonical Promotion Gates]]. These pages exist and are visible in the wiki. But are flagged as lower trust. So are not able to be cited by other pages as a direct source of information, and are not retrieved as primary pages during queries and retrievals. This is because the [[Confidence]] weighting average of all cited sources does not meet the defined minimum. So there is lower trust in the overall relevancy of the information
