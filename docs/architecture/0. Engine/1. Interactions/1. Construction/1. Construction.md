The continuous construction, maintenance, rebuilding and updating of the LLM wiki occurs every time connectors sync new source material in for consumption/re-consumption.
Upon consumption of source material, each item can go through **Up to 5 passes**
- Each passes is it's own job that, depending on 
	- The confidence/weighting of the source
	- The relevancy of the material and the number of linking pages
	- The affect that it has on existing source material
- Will determine if the source page carries on to the next pass/job
